About

Hello! We're the Berkeley Political Review Tech Department. We're responsible for keeping the BPR website up to date, creating interactive charts and other visualizations, and helping with everything tech-related at BPR. This year, we're expanding our abilities and adding new projects to our docket, so be on the lookout for cool things to come. Check out our personnel to learn more about us and to get in contact.

Request a Data Visualization For Your Article!

Have a cool idea for an interactive graph? Want to spice up your article? Consider requesting an interactive data visualization. Created by the data analysts in the Tech Department, these visualizations can represent a wide variety of data, including election results, economic data, and population trends. Use this form to request a visualization. If you can, provide as much detail as possible, including what kind of visualization you want, what kind of data we'll be usuing, and how we can find that data.

Notify Us of a Website Issue

If you encounter a bug or an issue with the BPR website (e.g. improper styling, a page doesn't load, or a link is broken), please fill out this form to notify us. Once we take a look at the issue, we'll contact you to let you know that it's been fixed (or when it will be fixed). Website Redesign

We're in the process of redesigning and modernizing the BPR website. Along with updating the styling of the website with the Design Department, we're also expanding the technical capabilities of the website. This means better integration for Sound Cloud, Plotly Data Visualizations, and more! Please be patient as we work to modify the website.
